Caprese Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 80 grams of buffalo mozzarella balls
  • 1/2 tomato
  • 2 slices of Parma ham
  • 2 slices of naturally-fermented bread
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Basil to taste
  • Rucula to taste
  • Salt to tast
  • Black pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Slice the mozzarella and tomato
  2. Season the tomato with salt and pepper
  3. Place on the bread a layer of mozzarella, the parma ham, the sliced tomatoes, the basil and the rucula
  4. Season the rucula with salt and close the sandwich
  5. Butter the outside of one side of the bread
  6. Place the buttered side down in a pan and grill the sandwich
  7. Spread butter on the other side of the sandwich
  8. Grill the other side of the sandwich
  9. Now just serve! Enjoy your meal
