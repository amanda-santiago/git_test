Yakisoba Recipe

Ingredients

  • 300g yakisoba noodles
  • 1 big onion chopped in medium pieces
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 small bunch of broccoli
  • 1/2 small bunch cauliflower
  • 250 ml yakisoba sauce
  • 6 Tbsp. soy sauce
  • 400 g beef strips (mignon, duckling or rump steak)
  • 100 g champignon
  • 1 carrot cut diagonally
  • 4 chard leaves cut diagonally

Preparation

  1. Cook the noodles in salted water and set aside
  2. In a large pot, put the oil and saute the onion
  3. Add the beef, broccoli, cauliflower, carrots, Swiss chard, mushrooms, and season with soy sauce
  4. Pour in the yakisoba sauce and cook until the vegetables are cooked to al dente
  5. Add the noodles, mix well and serve immediately
